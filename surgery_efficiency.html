Equation Logic Matrix Block

This visualization displays your surgical efficiency footprint index. It isolates monthly accumulated Biohazard Waste weights from manifests, scales them by a 0.7 volume coefficient modifier, and divides that product by overall documented Surgical Case Hours.

Index Definition
(0.7 × Biohaz Lbs) ÷ Hours

Monthly Surgery Footprint Variations

Higher bar values indicate elevated biohazard waste outputs generated per active surgical case hour.

Interpretation Guide If a specific month shows a spike in height, it suggests a higher concentration of biological disposal per hour of operation, highlighting an opportunity to review segregation practices in the OR during that period.
Data Cross-Aggregation Because tracking IDs do not natively intersect between logs, calculations are automatically grouped and linked by shared month-and-year properties before computing formulas.
