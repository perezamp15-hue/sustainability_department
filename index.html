How Waste Sourcing is Monitored & Modeled

This environment compiles shipping logs to map systemic sustainability trends. Data synchronizes across a four-tier architecture:

1. Manifest Entry
Logistics teams assign tracking IDs, locations, weights, and categories at delivery docks.
2. Aggregation
Manifest details sync directly inside your shared database master spreadsheet tabs.
3. Compilation
Automated Python workflows validate and strip extra whitespace out of row indices daily.
4. Visual Analysis
Frontend frameworks break down volume summaries and stack annual variables interactively.
