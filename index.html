How Waste Sourcing is Monitored & Modeled

This asset parses raw structured manifests directly tied to shipping logs. Data flows through a four-tier synchronization model:

Step 1: Manifesting
Logistics items get assigned individual tracking IDs, sites, weights, and category tags at processing docks.
Step 2: Aggregation
Manifest entries get compiled inside your shared Keck Medicine database workbook tabs.
Step 3: Compilation
Automated Python jobs extract records daily, standardizing whitespace variations across the system.
Step 4: Insights
The frontend transforms the structured data array to compute volume summaries on demand.
